# 🏡 Digital Land Registry (ERC-721)

Land ownership records are traditionally maintained through centralized databases and paper-based documentation, making them prone to inefficiencies, duplication, and difficult verification. This project explores how blockchain can serve as a transparent and tamper-resistant registry for recording and transferring property ownership.

The Digital Land Registry models a simplified government-backed land registration system where each property is represented by a unique ERC-721 token. Every token acts as a digital land title, permanently linked to a single property and its associated details, including the survey number, city, area, and metadata URI. The government acts as the trusted registrar, responsible for issuing new land titles, while ownership is managed through the ERC-721 standard.

By combining standardized NFT ownership with application-specific land records, the project demonstrates how real-world assets can be uniquely identified, securely owned, and transparently transferred on-chain.
